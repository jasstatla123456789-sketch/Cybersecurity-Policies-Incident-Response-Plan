# Executive Summary

## Project Title

**Cybersecurity Policies & Incident Response Plan for a Small Business**

---

## Overview

Cybersecurity has become a critical concern for businesses of all sizes. Small businesses are particularly vulnerable to cyber threats due to limited budgets, fewer IT resources, and a lack of formal security policies. Cybercriminals frequently target these organizations through phishing attacks, ransomware, malware, insider threats, weak passwords, and unsecured networks.

This project presents a comprehensive cybersecurity framework designed specifically for a small business. It provides practical security policies, risk assessment methods, incident response procedures, business continuity strategies, and disaster recovery planning to help organizations protect their digital assets and maintain secure business operations.

---

## Purpose

The primary purpose of this project is to establish a structured cybersecurity program that enables a small business to prevent, detect, respond to, and recover from cyber incidents while minimizing operational and financial risks.

---

## Objectives

The project aims to:

- Develop comprehensive cybersecurity policies.
- Identify and evaluate organizational risks.
- Protect confidential business information.
- Improve employee cybersecurity awareness.
- Create an effective Incident Response Plan.
- Establish Business Continuity procedures.
- Develop Disaster Recovery strategies.
- Align security practices with industry standards.

---

## Project Components

This project includes the following major components:

### 1. Risk Assessment

A detailed assessment identifies:

- Business assets
- Potential threats
- Existing vulnerabilities
- Risk levels
- Recommended security controls

---

### 2. Cybersecurity Policies

The project develops policies covering:

- Password Management
- Access Control
- Acceptable Use
- Data Protection
- Email Security
- Internet Usage
- Remote Work
- Backup Management
- Physical Security

---

### 3. Incident Response Plan

A structured response plan defines how the organization should:

- Detect incidents
- Report incidents
- Analyze security events
- Contain threats
- Remove malicious activity
- Recover business operations
- Document lessons learned

---

### 4. Business Continuity Plan

Business continuity procedures ensure that critical business functions remain operational during cyber incidents through:

- Backup operations
- Alternative communication methods
- Critical service prioritization
- Employee responsibilities

---

### 5. Disaster Recovery Plan

The disaster recovery section focuses on restoring IT infrastructure after major disruptions by defining:

- Backup strategies
- Recovery priorities
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO)
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO)
- System restoration procedures

---

## Benefits

Implementing this cybersecurity framework provides several advantages:

- Improved information security
- Reduced cyber risk
- Better regulatory compliance
- Increased customer trust
- Faster incident response
- Reduced downtime
- Protection against ransomware
- Improved employee awareness
- Enhanced business resilience

---

## Standards and Best Practices

This project is based on internationally recognized cybersecurity frameworks, including:

- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
- ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management System
- CIS Critical Security Controls
- OWASP Top 10 Security Risks

These standards help ensure that the policies and procedures follow modern cybersecurity best practices.

---

## Expected Outcomes

Upon implementation, the organization is expected to achieve:

- Stronger cybersecurity governance
- Better protection of confidential information
- Improved incident handling capabilities
- Reduced operational disruptions
- Enhanced preparedness against cyberattacks
- Increased employee awareness of security responsibilities

---

## Conclusion

Cybersecurity is no longer optional for small businesses. Even organizations with limited resources can significantly improve their security posture by implementing well-defined cybersecurity policies and an effective incident response process.

This project provides a practical, scalable, and easy-to-implement cybersecurity framework that helps protect business assets, ensures operational continuity, and prepares organizations to respond effectively to evolving cyber threats.
