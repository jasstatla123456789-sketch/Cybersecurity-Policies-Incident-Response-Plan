# Cybersecurity Policies & Incident Response Plan for a Small Business

## Abstract

This project presents a cybersecurity framework designed for small businesses. It includes security policies, risk assessment, incident response procedures, disaster recovery planning, business continuity strategies, and employee security awareness. The objective is to help organizations protect sensitive data, reduce cyber risks, and improve resilience against cyber threats.

---

## Introduction

Small businesses are increasingly targeted by cybercriminals because they often lack dedicated security teams and formal cybersecurity policies. This project provides practical security guidelines and an incident response plan based on industry best practices such as the NIST Cybersecurity Framework and ISO/IEC 27001.

---

## Objectives

- Develop cybersecurity policies.
- Protect business assets.
- Reduce cybersecurity risks.
- Improve employee awareness.
- Create an effective incident response plan.
- Ensure business continuity.
- Establish disaster recovery procedures.

---

## Scope

The project covers:

- Password Security
- Access Control
- Email Security
- Data Protection
- Backup Policy
- Remote Work Security
- Incident Response
- Risk Assessment
- Business Continuity
- Disaster Recovery

---

## Conclusion

Implementing cybersecurity policies and a structured incident response plan helps small businesses improve security, reduce financial losses, protect customer information, and maintain business operations during cyber incidents.
